Introducing AayuBot, an advanced chatbot designed to transform how we diagnose medical conditions. By simply providing the name of a medicine, users gain access to a comprehensive repository of medical knowledge. AayuBot integrates data from multiple extensive datasets, ensuring that users receive not only potential diagnoses but also detailed information about medicines, including their uses, side effects, and dosage recommendations. This cutting-edge system is designed to enhance healthcare accessibility and efficiency, bridging the gap between medicine names and actionable medical insights.


A UNIQUE FEATURE allows travelers to find equivalent medications across different regions, ensuring uninterrupted care. The system leverages Python and NLP technologies for an intuitive user experience, prioritizing data privacy and security.


Other UNIQUE FEATURE is that diagnose comes with a warning, showing type of medicine they are using (i.e. Antibiotics, Antacids, etc.).
